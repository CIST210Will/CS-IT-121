Garlic Parmesean Pasta

A dish that came from not having a dish!

  • a handful of pasta (eye measurement)
  • 4 cups of water or until your pot is half full
  • 1/2 cup of Parmesean cheese
  • 3/4 stick of butter or margarine
  • 1 tablespoon of onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon of thyme
  1. Bring your water to a boil
  2. boil your pasta for 10-12 min
  3. Drain your pasta once its tender
  4. Place pasta back in pot and add your butter
  5. Once your butter has melted turn the heat on low
  6. Add your remaining ingredients and fold/stir
  7. turn heat off and serve

This is my particular fave side dish because its one of those dishes that comes in handy when you don't have one. Most times these ingredients are readily available in the pantry.
